Racial Disparity in COVID Test Sites

Access to testing sites is step zero and must be taken to begin COVID response and treatment. Therefore, it is an essential resource that should be equally as accessible for all communities. However, in some counties of large ethnically Hispanic communities, this is not the case in that, there are often-times fewer testing sites in counties of similar sizes but with larger Hispanic population densities.
